🌟 My Magical Adventure

Writing Prompt: Imagine you found a magical door in your backyard. What's behind it? Describe your adventure! Think about what you see, hear, and feel.

💡 Writing Tips:

  • Use describing words (adjectives) to make your story interesting
  • Tell us what you see, hear, and feel
  • Use "I" to tell your story
  • Don't worry about perfect spelling - focus on your ideas!
Example Start: "When I opened the magical door, I saw a shimmering forest with purple trees and singing flowers..."
